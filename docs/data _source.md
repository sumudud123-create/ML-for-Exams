 Data Source Inventory — ML-for-Exams

1. Official Examination Papers
Source: Department of Examinations, Sri Lanka  
Description: Past G.C.E. Ordinary Level Science examination papers (2010–2023).  
Format:PDF (official printed versions)  
Access Method: Obtained through formal collaboration and data access agreement with the Department of Examinations.  
Storage: Encrypted local drive (not uploaded to GitHub).  
Usage:Used only for data extraction and analysis within this research.  

 2. Provincial Examination Papers
Source: Provincial Departments of Education (Western, Central, Southern Provinces).
 Description: Sample regional mock examination papers aligned with the national syllabus.  
  Format:PDF  
  Access Method:Provided by partner schools and regional education offices.
  Storage: Offline secure storage.  
  Usage:Supplementary dataset to improve model robustness and question diversity.

 4. Curriculum and Syllabus Documents
Source: National Institute of Education (NIE), Sri Lanka  
  Description: Official G.C.E. O/L Science syllabus (English Medium).  
  Access Method: Publicly available curriculum documents from NIE website.  
  Link: [https://nie.lk](https://nie.lk)  
  Usage: Used for curriculum alignment and labeling competency domains.

5.Grade 10 & 11 Science text books (English Medium)
Source : Ecucational Publications Department , Sri lanka
Description: Recomended and approved text books for Schools using in teaching learning process.
Access Method: Piblicty available textbooks documents from Educational Publications Department website.
Link : [https://www.edupub.gov.lk
Usage : Used for extract relevent text to generate questions

 6. Open Educational Datasets (for replication / demonstration)
Dataset:** SciQ – AI2 Science Questions  
Source:** [https://allenai.org/data/sciq](https://allenai.org/data/sciq)  
Description:** Open-access science question dataset used for model testing and replication in public GitHub code.  
Usage:** For reproducible experiments and demonstration (no confidential content).  



All official examination papers are accessed under the ethical approval of the research institution and data-sharing agreements.  
No raw question paper content or identifiable examination material is uploaded or distributed in this repository.
